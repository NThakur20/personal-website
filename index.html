A Little bit about me...

Hi, I am Nandan Thakur, currently working as a Data Scientist (RnD) at KNOLSKAPE, Bangalore. I'm an engineering graduate from the Birla Institute of Technology and Science, Pilani (BITS Pilani), India in Electronics Engineering with a minor in Finance. I am particularly interested in the resonance between real-world problems and data-powered solutions. I enjoy playing with data and my interests revolve primarily around data mining, Natural Language Processing and machine learning.

A large part of my Data Science journey lies within Electronics, followed by Finance and Biological Sciences. I am always very excited for novel learning opportunities across fields where I can use mathematical, technological, creative and leadership skills in tandem to solve real-world problems. I live my life on a quote - “All data has its beauty, but not everyone sees it. Those who see it, is after their willingness to feel stupid every single day at work.”

A Passionate Chelsea Fan, I love football and I occasionally go for a swim to rejuvenate myself. I also like to travel around and would never say no to a jog, marathon or any outdoor sport activity!
