Nandan Thakur

Hello! I am a Natural Language Processing (NLP) Researcher @ Ubiquitous Knowledge Processing (UKP) Lab at Technische Universität Darmstadt, Germany. Where I am advised by Prof. Dr. Iryna Gurevych and co-mentored by Nils Reimers and Johannes Daxenberger.

Currently exploring opportunities for MS/Ph.D. programs in Computational Linguistics and NLP (Fall Semester 2021) in Europe/U.K. region. Feel free to contact me on any of my social handles mentioned at the bottom or email me here.

My research interests are in the study of computational approaches to various linguistic questions. I love to take on broad and impactful linguistic questions and try to solve them with a simple to use and easily reproducible code -- impacting a bigger reach of audience. I devote my time to a mixture of both research and engineering as I have experience working in both. This is in synergy with my current position where I contribute engineering solutions as an employee of the ArgumenText group and additionally work on research-based linguistic questions that excite me, using Sentence Embeddings and Transformer architectures. I have a first-author paper currently under review in EMNLP 2020. (Let's hope for the best!)

Personally, I love football and I'm a passionate Chelsea FC supporter, Never miss a single game. Hardcore meat-lover, I love to cook occasionally and travel unknown beauties. I prefer nature over city. I love unplanned impromptu hikes, bike trips, or treks. I find gyms really-boring (no offense!) and I try to balance my fitness with running, cycling, and swimming (learning still, nearly there!).

Enough about me! Feel free to mention a few words about you.

I speak English, Hindi and Bengali fluently. I am learning German (A2).

Previous Experience

I previously worked as a Junior Data Scientist in KNOLSKAPE, Bangalore, India. During my undergraduate studies, I got two exciting research internship opportunities which played a crucial part in motivating NLP as my career path - First, an extensive research internship in probabilistic-based NLP mentored by Vikash Singh (Current: Google) in Belong.co, India. Next, an exciting Interdisciplinary project with Computational Biology advised by Dr. Toby Gibson and mentored by Manjeet Kumar in EMBL Heidelberg, Germany.

I completed my undergraduate studies from BITS Pilani, India majoring in Electronics Engineering with a minor in Finance. (shocked right?) I gradually transitioned into NLP and Deep Learning by learning on the flow while working and changed many verticals to pursue my interest - Electronics || Finance || Data Analytics || Data Science || NLP and Deep Learning. I strongly believe in hands-on experience benefits you faster than textbook study early in your career, something analogous to how I learned Football - “We were given the football to kick from day 1, learned ourselves by playing it. We didn't require to study a textbook to learn Football.”
